Good to Know

Is this an investment or a donation?

This is a rewards-based crowdfunding campaign — you're pre-ordering perks and supporting a local business, not purchasing equity or a financial return.

When will I get my reward?

Merch and coffee-bag rewards ship shortly after the campaign closes. Free-drink and discount rewards activate once our doors open, hopefully early fall 2026.

What happens if the goal isn't met?

We'll be transparent with backers about progress throughout the campaign and communicate clearly about next steps if we fall short.
