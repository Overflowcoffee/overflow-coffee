Good to Know

Is this an investment or a donation?

Neither — this is a Founding Supporter program. You're paying in advance for specific rewards (drinks, merch, discounts) from Overflow Coffee once we open. You're not purchasing equity, a financial return, or making a charitable donation.

When will I get my reward?

Merch and coffee-bag rewards ship shortly after this program closes. Free-drink and discount rewards activate once our doors open, hopefully early fall 2026.

Where exactly will Overflow be located?
